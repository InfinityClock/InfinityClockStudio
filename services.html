Medical Centers

Problem: Overwhelmed front-desks handling scattered phone calls and lost physical intake forms.
System: Encrypted digital intake software mapped to real-time doctor availability dashboards.
Workflow : Patient Submits Form → System Validates Slot → Reception Notified.
Result: Secure, legally-compliant and totally silent clinic operations.

Tattoo Studios

Problem: Managing multiple artists across paper diaries and failing to collect design draw-fees.
System: Centralized artist schedule matrix with mandatory digital waiver signatures.
Workflow : Consult Request → Fee Captured → Artist Slot Locked → Waiver Sent.
Result: 100% staff utilization and legally protected daily chair time.

Beauty Salons

Problem: Constant phone interruptions disrupting haircuts and losing track of stylist margins.

Restaurants & Cafes
