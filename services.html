Photo Studios

Medical Centers

Problem: Overwhelmed front-desks handling scattered phone calls and lost physical intake forms.
System: Encrypted digital intake software mapped to real-time doctor availability dashboards.
Workflow : Patient Submits Form → System Validates Slot → Reception Notified.
Result: Secure, legally-compliant and silent clinic operations.

Tattoo Studios

Problem: Managing multiple artists across paper diaries and failing to collect design draw-fees.
System: Centralized artist schedule matrix with mandatory digital waiver signatures.
Workflow : Consult Request → Fee Captured → Artist Slot Locked → Waiver Sent.
Result: Maximized efficiency and a protected daily schedule.

Beauty Salons

Problem: Constant phone interruptions disrupting haircuts and losing track of stylist margins.
System: Silent self-serve portal connected exactly to shift availability grids.
Workflow : Customer Picks Service → Selects Stylist → Reminders Pushed.
Result: Maximized styling uptime and highly predictable daily cashflow.

Restaurants & Cafes

Problem: Bleeding margins to delivery apps and re-typing tickets into the kitchen POS.
System: Direct-to-consumer digital menu natively piped into private Kitchen Displays.
Workflow : Web Order Placed → Payment Verifies → Screen Pings Chef.
Result: Control 100% of your margins and drastically speed up the prep line.

Local Retail Shops

Problem: Overselling physical stock online because actual store inventory is disconnected.
System: A responsive digital storefront synced directly with your inventory management system.
Workflow : Customer Buys Item → Stock Decrements → Reorder Alert Triggers.
